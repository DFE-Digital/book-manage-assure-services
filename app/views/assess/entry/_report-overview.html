How to write the report

As an assessor you need to think about how the services meets each of the 14 points of the Service Standard, plus any recommendations or actions for the team.

In the report you will:

  • consider the service against each of the 14 Service Standard points
  • give a RAG (red, amber, green) status against each standard point, any rated amber or red will also need specified actions for the team
  • consider an overall outcome for the service. If any of the Standard points receive a red or amber rating, this will be reflected in the overall rating

When collaborating on the report, you could:

  • create your own notes during the assessment, then add your contributions to the relevant sections for each Standard in the final report
  • use the panel chat in Slack to collaborate on findings and recommendations

In the report, you will be able to see all contributions added by the panel.

What happens next

The lead assessor will then review the report before submitting it to the service assessment team.
